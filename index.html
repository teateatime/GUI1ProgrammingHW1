  • 3) I'm Vietnamese.
  • And here are some of the most famous/popular dishes within the Vietnamese Cultures that I like.

Pho
A bone broth typically made up of multiple herbs,
including but not limited to bean sprouts, basil, and green onions.
It can also contain meatballs, thin slices of beef that are medium rare, rice noodles, and is overall a popular dish worldwide.
I generally like eating pho mainly due to how easy it is to make it.

Bun Bo Hue
A spicy broth rice noodle dish that contains beef and multiple spices.
It also includes several herbs and vegetables like green onions, bean sprouts, and shallots.
I typically like it due to how it offers packs more of a kick in spicyness compared to pho.
